Frequently Asked Questions

What is Viora?

Viora is a platform that helps individuals and organizations manage and use our product features in a simple and organized way. It allows users to create organizations, manage subscriptions, and access powerful tools through a unified dashboard.

 How do I get started with Viora?
Getting started with Viora is simple:
  • Go to the Sign Up page and create your account.
  • Complete your profile information.
  • Choose a subscription plan that fits your needs.
  • Create or join an organization.
 Can I change my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time.

 How do appointment reminders work?

Reminders are sent automatically via SMS or Email based on your custom notification settings.

 Is my data secure?

Yes, We take data privacy and security seriously. All user data is protected using secure systems.
